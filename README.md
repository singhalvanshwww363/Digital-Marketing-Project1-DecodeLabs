## 🏛️ Content Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Problem-First Thought Leadership (LinkedIn)
> Stop selling the solution. Start validating the problem.

Content explains **WHY** a pain point exists rather than just announcing a course. Education buyers want to feel understood, not sold to.

- **Format:** Data-backed infographics, text posts, carousel slides
- **Example post:** *"Why 70% of CS graduates struggle to get their first job"*
- **CTA style:** Conversation starter — *"What's the #1 skill gap in your campus?"*

### Pillar 2 — User-Generated Content & Social Proof (Instagram)
> Real photos outperform professional shots.

Showcase authentic, 'imperfect' visuals of interns working on real Deep-Stack projects.

- **Format:** Reels, carousels, behind-the-scenes stories
- **Psychological trigger:** Peer validation — seeing peers ship real apps is the strongest social proof
- **CTA style:** *"Tag a friend who needs this"* / *"DM us to join the next batch"*

### Pillar 3 — The Art of the CTA (Both Platforms)
> Low-friction verbs. Social proof next to every button.

- **Rule:** Avoid "Submit." Use "Start," "Get," or "Claim."
- **LinkedIn CTA:** *"Start My Career Track — Verified by AICTE"*
- **Instagram CTA:** *"Claim Your Seat — Next Batch Starts July 7"*
- **Technique:** Breadcrumb — ask for small commitment before the big ask

## 📅 30-Day Content Strategy

### Week-by-Week Funnel

| Week | Theme | Hook | Format |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| **Week 1** | Problem Awareness | Theory vs. Practice Gap | LinkedIn Infographic + Instagram Reel |
| **Week 2** | Authority Building | Proving Legitimacy | LinkedIn MSME/AICTE Doc + Instagram Carousel |
| **Week 3** | Technical Value | Reciprocity (Free Value) | LinkedIn Code Post + Instagram BTS |
| **Week 4** | Conversion | FOMO & Urgency | LinkedIn CTA Post + Instagram Countdown |

### Daily Posting Schedule

| Day | Platform | Theme | Format | Trigger |
